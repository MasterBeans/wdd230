Target Audience

The target audience for this website are local people whose work and activities greatly depend on weather conditions. Farmers usually check the weather forecast for seedling, planting and even harvesting of crops. Ambulant vendors also needed weather updates to get the right items to bring during their field work. Engineers and contructions require good weather forecast to paln and manage projects. Students and teachers may benefit from updated weather reports and forecasts.

Personas

Mike

An engineer who works in public highways and pavement projects. Project completion is directly and signnificantly affected by precise weather updates and forecasts.

Person 2

A student who commutes daily from home to school. Many times she brings in her bag umbrella so that she will not to stuck when unforeseen rain happens. She usually carry her books and other school items.

Scenarios

  • A farmer preparing the land and soil for planting. He checks with the current bulletin and forecast the timing for planting. Rain is required to have enough water in the field to start planting rice and other staple crops.
  • Ambulant fish vendors decided to stay home after she found out that a heavy rain is coming. She decided to take her products to sell on the next day if rain is not forecasted.
  • Dried fish is one of the common local food produce by fisherfolk. The best dried fish products are those dried by the heat of the sun. These fisherfolks rely on weather updates in order to get the best dried fish products.
  • Environmental specialists decided to conduct ocular inspections on the hilly parts of the city. These hills are prone to soil erosion and landslides. They planned to make their activity safe. Using the Panahon Center weather updates they are able to plan out the safest date and time to perform their inspection activities.
  • Workers from rice mills have to dry the rice before milling them. During sunny days they use to sun to dry the rice in large amounts, however on rainy days they use artificial dryers. These dryers have limited capacity and takes time and resources. Panahon Center can help these workers to determine the schedule and production of these rice mills.
