About ORCHAM

The Ormoc City Chamber of Commerce and Industry (OrCham) is a non-stock, non-profit, non-government business organization comprised of small, medium, and large enterprises, and industry associations representing various sectors of business, all working together to foster a healthier local economy and improve the viability of business in the community.
